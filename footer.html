The SurveillanceRights research project is an initiative of the Information Policy Research Program (IPRP) at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto. It is funded by the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council and received support from the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada and the Identity, Privacy and Security Institute (IPSI) at University of Toronto. For any general questions about the project or comments please contact Andrew Clement. To report any technical difficulties, broken links or errors on the website, please contact the site administrator.
